# Product Strategy

The product starts as a simple modular platform for small and medium condominiums that still depend on WhatsApp, spreadsheets, paper, and scattered documents.

Initial value proposition:

- Organize official communication.
- Track tickets and occurrences.
- Centralize documents.
- Manage common area reservations.
- Give the syndic a simple operational dashboard.

The first MVP must not compete with a complete ERP. It must validate real usage and willingness to pay with a secure, simple, tenant-safe foundation.
